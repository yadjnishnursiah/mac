FAQ

How do I care for my Eat' Cupcakes?

It is important to keep your Eat' Cupcakes at room temperature. Do not refrigerate them as this will dry out the cake.

How long do Eat' Cupcakes stay fresh?

We encourage you to enjoy them at their freshest, on the day of purchase. If you plan to keep your cupcakes longer than two days, plastic wrap the box or place the cupcakes in an airtight container and freeze.

Do you sell mini cupcakes?

Yes, our bakery sells an assortment of minis available for walk-in guests. You may also advance order 1 dozen or more minis of the same flavor.

Can I order in advance?

Contact Us is the fastest way to order or have cupcakes delivered for small to medium sized orders up to 7 days in advance. For catering orders, custom decorations, layered cakes, minis and further scheduled out orders you may place an order through our catering site.

If I order in advance, can I order any of your flavors?

We can make all our flavors of the day. Check when ordering for flavors available on certain days of the week. So, if you are ordering for pickup or delivery on a Monday, please choose from our Monday flavors.

If I order in advance, do I have to wait in line to pickup my cupcake order?

Skip right to the front of the line! Just give our counter staff your name and they will take care of you.

Do you ship cupcakes by mail?

Eat' Cupcakes does not currently ship our cupcakes by mail. We do ship our cupcake mixes and other retail items. To place an order for retail shipping, order online or call our dedicated order line at (+230) 5905-6329.

Do all of your cupcakes contain dairy/sugar/peanuts?

Eat' Cupcakes offers Sugar Free, Gluten Free and Vegan versions of our Red Velvet cupcakes daily at all bakeries. We also now have a Gluten Free Lemon Blueberry cupcake available daily.
